AP State Beverage Corporation Ltd (APSBCL)

Description

In response to the need for greater control over the sale of liquor and its social and financial impact, the Government of Andhra Pradesh has decided to oversee and regulate retail liquor outlets within the state. To ensure transparency and efficiency in these operations, the government has implemented an advanced ICT solution. This system enables the Department to closely monitor and manage the functioning of liquor retail shops, ensuring compliance with regulations and enhancing operational oversight. The solution incorporates modern technologies to facilitate real-time tracking, reporting, and management, thereby promoting accountability and smooth operation of these retail outlets under government supervision.
